About this book recommendation project...


Welcome to our book recommendation system! We've curated a collection of the top 10,000 books from Goodreads' "Books Everyone Should Read At Least Once" list. Our system employs sophisticated machine learning techniques to provide personalized recommendations based on your preferences.

At the heart of our system lies TF-IDF (Term Frequency Inverse Document Frequency), a powerful text analysis method. By applying TF-IDF, we transform textual book descriptions into numerical vectors, enabling us to perform calculations on this data.

Once all book descriptions are vectorized, we construct a cosine similarity matrix. This matrix captures the similarity between each pair of books by computing the cosine angle between their respective vectorized descriptions. A higher cosine similarity indicates greater similarity between books.

We leverage this matrix to generate recommendations. When you enter a book title into our recommendation form, we locate the index of the specified book in the matrix and identify the 15 most similar books based on their indices. Then, we retrieve the information of these recommended books and present them to you in a sorted list.

Our goal is to provide you with tailored book recommendations that match your reading preferences, promoting continued exploration and enjoyment of literature.
